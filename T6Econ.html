Course Description

CA128 NETWORKS (57 Contact Hours––3 Credits)

This is a basic course in macroeconomic and microeconomic concepts. Topics
considered include the method of economics, supply and demand, the price
mechanism, money and the American banking system, national output and
national income, monetary and fiscal policies, specialization and trade, and the
various market structures
